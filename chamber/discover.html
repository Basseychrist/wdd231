Chamber of E-commerce: 2024 Statistical Overview

1. Membership Growth

  • 10% Annual Increase in Membership: Over the past year, the Chamber of E-commerce has seen a steady 10% growth in its membership, now representing over 1,500 businesses across various sectors of e-commerce.
  • 500 New Members: In 2024 alone, 500 new businesses joined the chamber, expanding the diversity of our network.

2. E-commerce Revenue Generated

  • $10 Billion+ in E-commerce Sales: Member businesses collectively generated over $10 billion in e-commerce sales in 2024, contributing significantly to the local and global economy.
  • 20% Growth in Online Sales: Businesses that are part of the chamber reported an average 20% growth in their online sales after utilizing chamber-provided resources and networking opportunities.

3. Job Creation & Economic Impact

  • 5,000 New Jobs Created: Member businesses created more than 5,000 jobs in 2024, fostering economic growth and supporting local communities.

5. Networking & Business Opportunities

  • 200+ Events Hosted: The chamber organized over 200 events throughout the year, including industry conferences, trade expos, and networking forums, providing invaluable opportunities for members to connect and collaborate.
  • 10,000 Business Connections Made: More than 10,000 business partnerships and collaborations were formed through chamber events and networking platforms.

6. Advocacy & Policy Support

  • Advocated for $50M in E-commerce Legislation: The chamber successfully advocated for e-commerce-friendly policies, securing over $50 million in legislative support aimed at fostering the growth of digital businesses.
  • 75% of Members Report Positive Policy Changes: 75% of members reported that chamber-backed policy changes positively impacted their business operations, especially in areas like tax regulations and digital trade.
